About Us

Our Services

At Arav Interiors, we provide a variety of interior design services to suit your needs. Whether you're planning a big renovation or need help with smaller projects, we’re here to create a space that’s perfect for you.

Our Services:

  • Residential Interior Design: We design personalized living spaces that reflect your style and personality. From entire home makeovers to refreshing a single room, we create comfortable, stylish, and functional spaces that make you feel at home.
  • Commercial Interior Design: We design smart and welcoming spaces for businesses, including offices, retail stores, and restaurants. Our focus is on creating an environment that looks great and supports your business goals.
  • Space Planning & Layout: We help make the most of your space with smart layout designs. Our team plans everything carefully to ensure that your home or business flows well and feels comfortable, while maximizing every inch of available space.
  • Lighting Design: Good lighting can transform any space. We create lighting plans that enhance the look and feel of your interiors, ensuring that your space is bright, functional, and perfectly suited to your needs.
  • Color Consultation: Choosing the right colors can be overwhelming. We provide expert color consultation services to help you pick the best shades and combinations that suit your taste and bring your space to life.
  • Project Management: We manage the entire design process from start to finish. Our team coordinates with contractors, oversees construction, and makes sure everything runs smoothly and on time, so you can relax while we handle the details.
